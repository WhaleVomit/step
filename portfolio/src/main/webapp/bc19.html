Battlecode 2019

Battlecode is an annual AI tournament run by MIT. Competitors form teams of up to 4 and write bots to compete against one another in themed games. 2019's theme was 'Crusade', in which two teams start out with some castles that can produce units to gather resources and attack the opponent. A castle that is destroyed cannot be rebuilt. The goal is to have the most castles surviving at the end of the match. Ties are broken by total unit value.

Due to the mechanics of the game, most teams employed defensive strategies that solely defended their own castles in hopes of winning in unit value. However, our team devised the infamous "church lightning" strategy which instantly builds a long chain of units, allowing us to infiltrate enemy bases in a resource-efficient manner.
