About me

Hello! My name is Lawrence and I am a rising sophomore at Carnegie Mellon University studying computer science. I've lived in Princeton, NJ since 7th grade.

Some of my hobbies are listening to music and playing video games. I also really enjoy games that require me to think hard, such as Tetris, poker, and Avalon. Recently, I've been playing a bit of chess.

One of my passions is competitive programming. This year I am a member of CMU's ICPC team. ICPC is an international programming contest with students from universities all around the world. Out of more than 3,000 total universities, CMU is one of the 128 advancing to the World Finals in Moscow.

I also have a cat named Pluto!
